About

ThoughtPad is an online auto-saving note taking web-app. In other words whatever you type in at ThoughtPad is
instantly saved in your browser(not on a server). So type in your thoughts and forget about it as all data is auto saved every 2 seconds. That said, if you clear your browser data/cookies/cache you lose all your data saved!

ThoughtPad is built and maintained by Sandeep Tripathy
